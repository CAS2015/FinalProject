About Voyager's Handbook

Voyager's Handbook was created by Erik Kell, Eric Paulson, Chelsey Sassine, and Thomas Webel as a final project for the Skill Distillery Java full-stack web development bootcamp. Chelsey had the idea for the app after her extensive world travels left her with knowledge of some strange requirements that certain countries impose on travelers.

We hope you enjoy the site and take away some useful tips!
